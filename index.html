I am an Assistant Professor at Beijing Institute of Technology

(my school page can be found here: BIT-page)

Previously, I was a Maxwell Institute Postdoctoral Research Fellow in Edinburgh.

I completed my PhD at the University of Edinburgh (MIGSAA CDT).

PhD thesis
Deep-water and shallow-water limits of the intermediate long wave equation: from deterministic and statistical viewpoints

Supervised by Prof. Tadahiro Oh and Prof. Yuzhao Wang

Research Interests

Nonlinear Partial Differential Equations and Harmonic Analysis. In particular, the study of deterministic and probabilistic nonlinear dispersive PDEs.

I have broadened my interests in probability theory through Guangqu Zheng (Boston University) and Jiawei Li (University of Edinburgh).

Non-maths Interests

I regularly work out at the gym, and my current gym buddy is Zhenyao Sun, a probabilist at BIT. I also enjoy hiking, and every summer I spend some time travelling on foot. My next planned trek is the Kumano Kodō in the summer of 2027.
